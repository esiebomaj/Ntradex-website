How ntradeX Uses Your Personal Information

This Privacy Policy explains how Ntradex collects, holds, uses, and discloses personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs). “Personal information” means information or an opinion about an identified individual, or an individual who is reasonably identifiable, including but not limited to name, address, email address, telephone number, date of birth, identification information, and financial information.

This Privacy Policy applies only to Ntradex and does not apply to the practices of third parties that Ntradex does not own or control.

Information We Collect

Ntradex may collect personal information when you:
• register for a Ntradex account
• use our products or services
• communicate with our support or sales teams
• participate in promotions or surveys
• interact with our website or mobile applications

 

Depending on the services you use, we may collect:
• full name
• date of birth
• residential or business address
• email address and phone number
• government-issued identification
• business registration details
• bank account or payment information
• transaction history
• device, IP address, and usage data

 

Where required by law, we may also collect information for identity verification and ongoing customer due diligence.

How We Use Your Information

Ntradex uses personal information for the following purposes:
• to provide and operate our products and services
• to verify identity and conduct customer due diligence
• to comply with legal and regulatory obligations
• to process transactions and manage accounts
• to communicate with users regarding their accounts
• to improve and develop our products and services
• to detect, prevent, and investigate fraud or illegal activity
• to produce aggregated and anonymised analytics

We only use personal information where it is reasonably necessary for our business operations or required by law.

 

Where required by law, we may also collect information for identity verification and ongoing customer due diligence.

Disclosure of Personal Information

Ntradex does not sell or rent your personal information.

We may disclose your personal information:
• to service providers who assist us in operating our platform
• to financial institutions, payment partners, and compliance providers
• where required or authorised by law or court order
• to regulators, including AUSTRAC, where legally required
• to investigate suspected fraud, illegal activity, or security incidents
• as part of a corporate transaction such as a merger, acquisition, or restructuring

 

Where personal information is disclosed to third parties, Ntradex takes reasonable steps to ensure those parties handle the information in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

Cross-Border Disclosure

Ntradex operates in multiple jurisdictions. In some cases, personal information may be disclosed or stored outside Australia.

Where this occurs, Ntradex takes reasonable steps to ensure overseas recipients handle personal information in a manner consistent with Australian privacy requirements.

Data Security

Ntradex maintains reasonable physical, technical, and administrative safeguards to protect personal information against misuse, interference, loss, unauthorised access, modification, or disclosure.

These safeguards include encryption, access controls, monitoring systems, and secure infrastructure.

No system is completely secure; however, Ntradex continuously reviews and improves its security measures.

Access and Correction

You may request access to, or correction of, your personal information held by Ntradex by contacting our support team.

We will respond to such requests within a reasonable timeframe, subject to legal and regulatory requirements.

Children Privacy

Ntradex services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from individuals under 18 years of age. In the event that we discover that an individual under 18 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers where legally permissible. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact our support team at support@ntradex.ca to take necessary actions.

Third-Party Links

Our services may contain links to third-party websites or services. Ntradex is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third parties. Users should review the privacy policies of any external sites they visit.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

ntradeX may update this policy at any given time. We will notify you about significant changes in our Privacy policy by sending a notice to the primary email you registered with us or by placing a prominent notice on our site.

Questions and Suggestions

If you have questions, suggestions, or wish to make a complaint, please send an mail to support@ntradex.ca.
