questions about ntradeX

New to ntradeX?

How can I get the ntradeX app

You can download the ntradeX app from Google Play Store (for Android devices) & Apple App Store (for iOS devices).
After installation, simply open the app and sign up using your phone number and email address to get started.


What documents do I need to submit to get my account?

To verify your account, simply submit/upload a government-issued ID card.


How long does it take to verify my account?

The process can be completed within minutes. If you experience any problem during the verification process, you can reach out to the support team on the app or email support@ntradex.ca.

Payments and Transfers

How can I send money internationally using ntradeX?

Simply log into your account, select the currency and amount you want to send, and enter the recipient’s details. We handle the rest, ensuring your money gets there quickly and securely.


What are the supported currencies for sending and receiving payments?

You can send and receive payments in NGN, USD, GBP, EUR, and CAD. Conversion options are also available for cross-currency transactions.


Can I exchange my currencies in the app?

Yes, currency swaps can be done within the app in just seconds! You can exchange currencies in your virtual accounts anytime.


How long do international transfers take?

Transfer times vary depending on the destination, but most transactions are completed within 30 minutes.


What is the transfer limit per currency?

Transaction limits depend on your account verification status.
There are 3 cadres of KYC verification. Your transaction limit depends on your cadre of verification.

Fees and Exchange Rates

Are there any transfer fees?

There are no transaction fees for NGN transactions. However, fees apply for other currency pairs. We provide a transparent breakdown of all fees before you confirm your transaction.


How are currency exchange rates determined?

Our exchange rates are dynamic an update hourly in real-time. You’ll see the rate applicable at the time of your transaction before confirming.


Are there any hidden charges?

No, we are committed to transparency. All applicable fees and charges are displayed upfront before you complete any transaction.

Customer Support

What should I do if my transaction fails?

If a transaction fails, you’ll receive a notification with the reason. Common causes include incorrect recipient details or insufficient balance. Contact our support team for further assistance if needed.


How can I contact customer support?

You can reach us through the in-app live chat feature or email support@ntradex.ca. Our support team is always ready to help.


What if the recipient hasn’t received the payment?

If the payment status shows “Completed” but the recipient hasn’t received it, please ask the recipient to check with their bank. If the issue persists, contact our support for assistance.
